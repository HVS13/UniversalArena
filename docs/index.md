## How To Play

1. Each player choose their characters. There cannot be the same character with the same Name and Version on one team.
2. Play divided into 3 phase. Turn Start, Combat Start, Combat Resolve, Combat End, Turn End.
3. At Turn Start, each player draw until they have 5 cards in hand and 5 energy.
4. At Combat Start, the player with initiative can play any card they want as long as they pay the cost. When they have enough, they decide to pass or end. Next phase only begin when both player decide to end.
    1. If pass, the next opponent can do the same until they decide to pass again or end.
    2. If end, the next opponent can do the same until they decide to end.
5. At Combat Resolve, begin to resolve normal speed card one by one from the top of the stacks to the bottom.
6. At Combat End, begin to resolve slow speed card one by one from the top of the stacks to the bottom.
7. At Turn End, all player discard all remaining cards and then pass the initiative to the next player.

## Playing Cards
1. To play any cards, pay the energy cost, select the legal target, then place them on the Combat as stack each according to their speed (normal/slow).
2. If the said cards have fast speed, the card will instantly be resolved without waiting to be resolved on the stack.
3. For each card played, you gain ultimate meter equivalent to the energy spent using the card +1.
