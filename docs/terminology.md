    <tr id="term-draw">
      <td>Draw</td>
      <td>The act of taking cards from your Draw Pile and adding them to your hand. By default, you draw at the start of your turn until you have 5 cards.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr id="term-discard">
      <td>Discard</td>

    </tr>
    <tr id="term-initiative">
