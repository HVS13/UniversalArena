<div class="keyword-entry" id="keyword-forte">
  <h3>Forte</h3>
  <p>Max 2 Count. Primary resource for Forte-based abilities.</p>
</div>

<div class="keyword-entry" id="keyword-spectro-frazzle">
  <h3>Spectro Frazzle</h3>
  <p>Max 10 Count. At the end of the opponent's turn, inflict damage equal to 5 * Count, then reduce Count by 1.</p>
</div>

<div class="keyword-entry" id="keyword-stagnant-potency">
  <h3>Stagnant Potency</h3>
  <p>Applies lingering damage that persists for several turns.</p>
</div>

<div class="keyword-entry" id="keyword-combo">
  <h3>Combo</h3>
  <p>Grants bonus damage when triggered after another card of the same type this turn.</p>
</div>

<div class="keyword-entry" id="keyword-break">
  <h3>Break</h3>
  <p>Temporarily lowers an enemy's defenses, increasing damage taken.</p>
</div>

<div class="keyword-entry" id="keyword-channel">
  <h3>Channel</h3>
  <p>Spend the current turn to prepare a stronger effect on the next round.</p>
</div>
